## Series Selection Note

On `LJ71C24 + L26CPU-BT`, `--series ql` is the practical setting.

Observed follow-up behavior:

- `--series ql`: contiguous access and native `0403/1402/0406/1406/0801/0802` pass
- `--series iqr`: even contiguous `D100` / `M100` reads returned `0x7F22`
