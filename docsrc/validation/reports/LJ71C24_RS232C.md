Audience: maintainers and users who need the current validated view of the `LJ71C24` path.

## Target

- Link: `RS-232C`

## Series Selection Note

On `LJ71C24 + L26CPU-BT`, `--series ql` is the practical setting. `--series iqr` is not a practical
mode on this target because even contiguous `D100` / `M100` reads returned `0x7F22`.
