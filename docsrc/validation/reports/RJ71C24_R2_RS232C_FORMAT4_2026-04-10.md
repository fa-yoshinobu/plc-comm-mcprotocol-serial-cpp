### Sum-check follow-up

The validated setup remains `sum-check off`.

- `read-words` with `sum-check off`: pass
- `read-words` with `sum-check on`: `0x7F23`

### Client-side parsing guard

During follow-up diagnostics, one isolated `1402` bit attempt appeared to succeed when a non-empty success-data frame was accepted by the host side.
That path is now guarded in the client: write commands and monitor registration reject unexpected response data instead of treating it as success.
